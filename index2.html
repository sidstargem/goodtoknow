About Us

Established in June 2021, Javitech has grown from strength to strength as a focused IT value-added reseller. Our aim is to deliver exceptional service to our customers in corporate and public sector organisations.

Whether you require developing a specific technology strategy or just looking for some knowledgeable and friendly guidance, we maintain top-tier accreditations to ensure we can bring you the best possible expertise and pricing. We can assure you our alignment with leading vendors, such as HP Inc, HPE, Dell Technologies, Cisco, Microsoft, Lenovo, Citrix and VMware, does not affect our advice. We pride ourselves on being vendor-agnostic both in our thinking and strategy, and we will only ever advise and propose what is right for you.

The success of your projects is what drives us, our people and operations are second to none, ensuring we listen to you and deal with your enquiry quickly and efficiently, irrespective of the size of your business or value of your order.
